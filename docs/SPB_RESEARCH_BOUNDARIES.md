# SPB Research Boundaries

This repository is a controlled research sandbox for Firestarter Structural Participation Breakdown work.

SPB means Structural Participation Breakdown. It is a research candidate only.

## Scope

Allowed work:

- Historical market data collection for research review.
- Binance and Bitget futures data audits.
- Candle, funding, open-interest, and positioning data availability checks.
- Timestamp, row-count, and source-quality audits.
- Research reports and manifests.

Not allowed in this sandbox:

- Live market execution.
- Production automation.
- Account actions.
- API keys or secrets committed to the repository.
- Raw scanner mutation.
- Strategy promotion.
- Performance claims.
- Unreviewed decision labels.

## Data handling

Raw market data should stay local in controlled data folders. Do not copy raw market data into Notion, Drive, Slack, or chat transcripts.

All data files must preserve source, symbol, interval, fetch timestamp, and UTC timestamp fields where possible.

## Review gate

Any research output must pass Jody audit before being interpreted as useful evidence.
